Personal Projects

Do something that you want to do. I do not doubt tutors when they say we have it easy with the flexibility of briefs given in class. But even then it may not be exactly what you are craving. Break it up with self-motivated projects.

1. Instagram challenges are a great way to keep you active and doing something new everyday. The (fill in the blank) a day challenge is a simple way to evolve your skills (or 30 days if you want to start smaller). Whether it is a logo, poster, illustration, etc.

2. Explore prompt generators to kickstart possible projects. They will keep you on your toes and your creative methods fleixible. Designercize provides purposeful briefs whereas Sibisibi is more artistic.
